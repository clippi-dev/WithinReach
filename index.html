WithinReach - The Sailing Journal

The first time I went sailing on the San Francisco Bay was with Jim McGihon. Jim was my boss at GE Capital Genstar Container Corporation. I was hooked from that first Friday afternoon where we cut out of work early. The things I enjoyed were:

  • All you think about is which way the wind is blowing
  • It's technical with infinite ability to learn
  • Never done learning
  • Scenic
  • Relaxing

It was a short excursion, leaving from Sausilito, CA in the early afternoon. As I recall, we only sailed to the Golden Gate Bridge and back, but I was hooked.
